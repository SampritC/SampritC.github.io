VEB: Simulation Overview

This page collects simulation results for a mean-field variational empirical Bayes procedure for high-dimensional Gaussian linear regression. The method learns a discrete prior on a grid and produces a fast posterior-mean estimator of $\beta$. All methodological and theoretical details are in the project PDF; here we only display empirical behavior.
